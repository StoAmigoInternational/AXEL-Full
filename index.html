If you haven't heard of Blockchain...

Digital Ledger

It's essentially a public, digital ledger that keeps records of anything built with its technology.

From A to B

Imagine a document is shared using blockchain. It goes from person A to person B to person C.

Up the chain

Blockchain allows you to trace that chain all the way back to person A. You'll know where the file came from and its complete transaction history. It's like a digital signature for a file that ensures it is what it says it is.

FAQ

  • How are you planning to make money from this?

    Yes, we do actually have a plan to make money. That's a good thing - companies that don't make money don't stick around.
    A small percentage will be charged per transaction - one far smaller than hosting a storefront or using PayPal. But consider that there will be billions, maybe trillions of file transactions happening. It adds up.
    More, people will be able to choose to view advertising for tokens. Or get preferred listings in the markets for a larger percentage.
    And there are other, even MORE exciting opportunities in the works.

  • Explain this to me in plain English.
    1. We've built a multi-platform file sharing and transfer app that tons of people use. It's called StoAmigo. It's neat.
    2. We've introduced blockchain technology to our app to create a new feature called [Transactional Share]. Essentially, it allows you to sell files with a link - only you don't get money. You get AXEL Tokens instead.
    3. You can exchange AXEL Tokens for local currency, and vice-versa.
    4. We plan to build a searchable marketplace for people who are selling files that anyone can join.
  • Why use AXEL Tokens instead of, say, cash?

    There are several very good reasons to use AXEL Tokens instead of cash.

    1. AXEL Tokens are built with blockchain technology. This allows HUGE advantages over traditional transactions. Such as instant, simultaneous transfer. A complete record of transactions, which allows for built-in IP protection for files. It's encrypted for the highest levels of privacy. And many other things, some of which we're still working on.
    2. The marketplace will be a global destination, and the token needs to account for that. People from every country will participate, and they can't all use their own currency or it'd be a mess. A thousand different exchange rates, regulations, and so on. AXEL lets us use one method of exchange that makes things simple.
    3. Tokens that are no beholden to any particular country mean the marketplace will be free from local regulations and censorship.
  • Why is the AXEL ICO different from other ICOs?

    Because we have more than an idea - we have what we need to succeed. And look, that's not to say there isn't some risk involved. When it comes to ICOs, there will ALWAYS be some risk. But some are riskier than others, and you can tell which ones by their fundamentals.

    1. We have an experienced team that has built large, profitable companies from the ground up. They still exist today. Several people on our executive team are successful entrepreneurs, and know how to make things happen. We have combined CENTURIES of knowledge and industry skills.
    2. We have a live app that you can download today, across multiple devices. Windows, Mac, Android, iOS, and web. It works, and it's had millions of downloads with ratings above 4.4/5. We're not starting from scratch.
    3. We have patented technology that no one else in the world can replicate. That means the way we're doing things cannot be "copied" by anyone else. Not some newfangled startup, not even large corporations. It's unique and only we can do it.
  • Why use block chain?

    Blockchain allows for built-in encryption and a level of decentralization that is absolutely critical to the peer-to-peer ecosystem we're trying to build. Blockchain is the natural next step in our mission of giving people back control of their stuff.
